So what is this for and why?

This is honestly just a basic project to build a Flask app whose main purpose is to display webscraped data.

I wasn't sure what to pull from each site or what kind of sites I wanted to scrape. Seeing as my current occupation is at a laser company, there was a thought to scrape laser prices, but unfortunately, that's not something readily available to the public.

After much (too much) deliberation on the matter, I decided to scrape data on the top Spotify songs from Wikipedia. It's not a perfect project—if such a thing even exists—but it was a fun way to practice and a great learning experience.
